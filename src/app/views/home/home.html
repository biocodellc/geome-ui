The Genomic Observatories Meta-Database (GEOME) is a web-based database that captures the who, what, where, and when of biological samples and associated genetic sequences. GEOME helps users with the following goals:

  • Ensure the metadata from your biological samples is findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable
  • Improve the quality of your data and comply with global data standards
  • Integrate with R, ease publication to NCBI's sequence read archive, and work with an associated LIMS
